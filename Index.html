Stock Market Simulator

What is a stock Market Simulator?

A stock Market Simulator is a virtual Game in which students are be able to invest unreal money and gain experience about the stock market interactively. The top 100 stocks from the New York Stock Exchange and NASDAQ are displayed in which players will be able to trade their stocks and grow their portfolio. Players can also collect some important information about the stock market from our persnalised newsfeed.

Who is this Game for?

This game is made for high school students who are interested in learning more about economics, and the stock market. This game provides them with understanding of the stock market and how they can better use their funds

How does it Work?

A player gets to invest in the top 100 stocks from NASDAQ and NYSE, they can sell the stocks when they see good value in them and grow their portfolio. They can buy more stocks when the value is down, to make more profit! To buy a stock simply click the 'buy' button, and to sell a stock click the 'sell' button

Aim of the game

This game aims to help the students be more involved in what they learn, through this game they see the real-world applications to their theoritical learning done in classrooms.

Why is this game important?

Learning about stock markets helps the students understand investing in their long term plans. Which would help them grow their wealth. Teaching students about the stock market gets them mentally prepared for their personal finance. It also provides a better understanding to the students about how a comapny functions and how their value can fluctuate.
